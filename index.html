Bon Voyage with BTS

BTS 본보야지 (BTS: Bon Voyage) is a reality by the boy group BTS. The reality features the members of BTS travelling around the world. The first season aired on July 5, 2016, took place in Norway, Sweden and Finland for over 10 days, and it was a gift for their third anniversary. The second season aired on June 27, 2017, took place in Hawaii for over 9 days. The third season aired on September 18, 2018, took place in Malta, Europe. The four season aired on November 19, 2019, and took place in New Zealand.
